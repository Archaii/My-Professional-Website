Instructions for adding your images:

1. LOGO:
   - Save your logo as "logo.png" in this folder
   - Recommended size: 36x36 pixels (will be automatically resized)
   - Supported formats: PNG, JPG, or WebP
   - The logo will appear in the header and footer of all pages

2. PROFILE PICTURE:
   - Save your profile picture as "profile-pic.jpg" in this folder
   - The image should be square (recommended: 400x400 pixels or larger)
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, or WebP
   - The image will be automatically resized and styled with a circular border

The website is already configured to display both your logo and profile picture.
