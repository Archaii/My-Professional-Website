Boost: Career Roadmap Generator

Mobile App AI/ML Career Development Completed

An intelligent mobile application that creates personalized career roadmaps based on your skills, interests, and goals. Get step-by-step guidance, resource recommendations, and track your progress towards your dream career.

Project Overview

Boost is a smart career development companion that uses AI to generate personalized career roadmaps. Whether you're a student exploring career options, a professional looking to switch fields, or someone wanting to upskill, Boost provides a clear, actionable path to achieve your career goals with curated resources and milestone tracking.

Why This Project?

Many students and professionals struggle to navigate career paths and skill development. Boost addresses this by providing personalized, AI-generated roadmaps that break down complex career goals into manageable steps, making career development accessible and achievable for everyone.
